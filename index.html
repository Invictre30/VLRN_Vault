About Me

Princess
Valerie

Multimedia Artist · Graphic Designer

I'm Princess Valerie, a multimedia artist student based in Trece Martires City, Cavite, Philippines. I specialize in graphic design, branding, and visual storytelling.

I enjoy composing work that feels intentional and expressive. A perfect balance of clean visuals with emotion and concept. Most of my works are inspired by creative storytelling, mood, and minimal details that make the design stand out.

Outside designing, I love exploring new styles and experimenting with different creative ideas.

Feel free to browse my work or reach out if you'd like to collaborate.
