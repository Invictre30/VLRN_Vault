01 — Overview

A Universe of Expression

Morphéa is a Philippine cosmetics brand built on the idea that every person deserves to transform — to glow, to shift, to become limitless.

The brand draws on cosmic imagery: nebulae, starfields, and the fluid energy of the universe. Deep purples, midnight blues, and electric pinks form the visual language of a brand that feels both otherworldly and intimate.

The brand system spans logo marks, a full color palette and typography guide, product packaging, shopping collateral, and marketing materials — all unified by the same galactic soul.

02 — Logo System

The Mark of Transformation

The Morphéa logo system consists of three distinct marks — each expressing the same fluid, cosmic identity at different scales and contexts.

Primary Logo

The complete Morphea logo featuring the signature calligraphic heart emblem integrated with the primary wordmark. This version serves as the main brand identifier and should be used across digital platforms, packaging, marketing materials, and official brand communications.

Click to expand ↗

Logo Concept Sketch

Original hand-drawn exploration of the Morphea identity. This concept sketch documents the creative development process behind the heart symbol and custom lettering, showcasing the evolution from initial idea to finalized brand mark.

Light Logo Variant

An alternative version of the Morphea logo optimized for light backgrounds and high-contrast applications. Designed to maintain legibility and visual consistency across print materials, packaging, and collaborative brand environments.

✦

The calligraphic heart mark is always rendered with the gradient — never in flat black alone.

✦

Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the "M" in the wordmark on all sides.

✦

Do not rotate, stretch, or recolor the mark outside approved brand palette.
